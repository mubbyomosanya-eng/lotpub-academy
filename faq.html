Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting

Do I need any prior experience?
No. The Beginner course starts from absolute zero. You don't need to know anything about coding before joining.
What equipment do I need?
A laptop or desktop computer (Windows/Mac/Linux) is ideal. A smartphone can work for the beginner level, but a laptop is recommended for intermediate and pro courses.
How are classes delivered?
All classes are on WhatsApp. You receive recorded video lessons, written notes, coding challenges, and live Q&A sessions. You can learn at your own pace.
What if I miss a class or fall behind?
All materials stay in the WhatsApp group forever. You can catch up anytime, and the instructor will answer your questions when you return.
Do I get a certificate after completion?
Yes. After finishing the course and submitting your final project, you receive a verifiable certificate of completion.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes, flexible payment plans are available. Contact us to discuss what works best for you.
How do I get started?
Click the "Enroll Now" button on any page, or message us on WhatsApp. We'll guide you through the registration and payment process.
